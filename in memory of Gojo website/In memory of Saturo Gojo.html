Satoru Gojo,
the life the love and the facts



Born into a family with a rich lineage of jujutsu sorcerers, Satoru Gojo emerged as a prodigious talent from a young age. Raised in the intricate world of curses and jujutsu, Gojo displayed unparalleled potential in mastering Cursed Techniques and harnessing Cursed Energy. As he grew older, Gojo's reputation as a powerful sorcerer solidified, eventually leading him to become a teacher at Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical High School. His carefree and irreverent attitude masks a profound commitment to protecting humanity from the malevolent forces of curses. Gojo's prowess in combat is legendary, making him a feared adversary among curses. His mastery over Limitless Cursed Technique and Six Eyes, coupled with his strategic brilliance, positions him as the strongest sorcerer in the Jujutsu Kaisen universe. Beyond his combat skills, Gojo assumes the role of mentor to the school's students, including guiding the protagonist, Yuji Itadori. His enigmatic past and complex motivations add depth to his character, shaping him into a central figure in the ongoing battle against the supernatural threats that loom over the world of Jujutsu Kaisen.
